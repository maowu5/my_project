Harvey

Harvey is the town doctor. He’s a little old for a bachelor, but he has a kind heart and a respected position in the community. Harvey cares about the well-being of the villagers and enjoys talking about medicine and science.

Abigail

Abigail is energetic and adventurous. She’s not the type to sit around inside all day. Abigail loves to explore the wilderness and has a strong interest in the paranormal. She often talks about her interest in venturing into the mines.

Leah

Leah is a talented artist who loves to spend time in nature. She enjoys sculpting and painting, and her cabin in the woods reflects her creative and rustic lifestyle. Leah can often be found wandering in the forest or by the river.

Sebastian

Sebastian is a reclusive programmer who spends most of his time indoors working on his computer. He’s also interested in comic books, role-playing games, and motorcycles. Sebastian often struggles with his desire to escape small-town life.

Maru

Maru is an intelligent and creative young woman. She loves working with machines and gadgets, and often helps her father in his lab. Maru enjoys tinkering and building robots, and she dreams of one day pursuing a career in engineering.

Penny

Penny is a quiet and shy woman who spends her time teaching the children of Pelican Town. She lives with her mother, Pam, in a small trailer and dreams of a life beyond the confines of the town. Penny enjoys reading and tutoring the kids.

Sam

Sam is an outgoing and carefree young man who loves skateboarding and playing guitar in his band. He dreams of becoming a professional musician one day. Sam is always upbeat and friendly, and he often gets into mischief with his friends.

Haley

Haley is the town's fashionista. She’s obsessed with clothes and makeup and dreams of moving to the city to pursue a career as a model or photographer. At first, Haley seems shallow, but she has a softer side that shows as you get to know her.

Alex

Alex is a confident and ambitious young man. He spends most of his time working out and playing sports, hoping to one day become a professional gridball player. Despite his tough exterior, Alex has a vulnerable side and harbors doubts about his future.

Emily

Emily is quirky and creative, and she enjoys sewing clothes and crafting items. She has a cheerful personality and often talks about dreams and the spiritual world. Emily works part-time at the Stardrop Saloon and is always looking to brighten someone’s day.

Elliott

Elliott is a writer who lives alone in a cabin on the beach. He’s dedicated to his craft and spends most of his time working on his novel. Elliott is well-read and often speaks poetically, reflecting his romantic and artistic nature.

Shane

Shane works at JojaMart and struggles with depression and alcoholism. He’s initially rude and closed off but opens up over time. Despite his rough exterior, Shane has a love for chickens and dreams of finding a happier life.

Jodi

Jodi is a hardworking mother who takes care of her two sons, Sam and Vincent. She is always busy with housework but dreams of a life where she can relax more. Jodi is kind-hearted and always makes sure her family is well-cared for.

Caroline

Caroline is the wife of Pierre and mother of Abigail. She spends a lot of time working in the garden and enjoys the outdoors. Caroline is kind and supportive of her family, though she wishes she had more time for herself.

Robin

Robin is the town’s carpenter and a busy working mother. She’s married to Demetrius and is the mother of Sebastian and Maru. Robin is a practical, hardworking woman who enjoys spending time in her workshop and helping the townspeople with their building projects.

Linus

Linus is a recluse who lives in a tent near the mountains. He prefers to live away from the rest of the town and values his independence. Despite his rough living conditions, Linus is a kind and philosophical person who enjoys nature.

Pam

Pam is a heavy drinker who lives in a trailer with her daughter Penny. She used to be the bus driver before the bus stopped running, and she struggles with her habits and financial situation. Pam has a tough personality but cares deeply for Penny.

Demetrius

Demetrius is a scientist who studies the natural world. He’s married to Robin and is the father of Maru and Sebastian. Demetrius is curious and loves conducting experiments. He often talks about his research on plants and animals.

Pierre

Pierre owns the local general store in Pelican Town. He’s always focused on his business and competes with JojaMart for customers. Pierre is a dedicated businessman and family man, but he can be a bit stingy when it comes to his store's success.

Evelyn

Evelyn is an elderly woman who has lived in Pelican Town her entire life. She’s kind and sweet, and she enjoys baking and gardening. Evelyn is married to George and takes great pride in her family and her flowers.
